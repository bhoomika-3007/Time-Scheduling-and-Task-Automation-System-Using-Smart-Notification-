MVN University Timetable Management

MVN University helps faculty, and students create, manage, and view class schedules with ease. Say goodbye to scheduling conflicts and time-consuming timetable creation.

Powerful Features

MVN University offers a comprehensive set of tools to streamline timetable management for everyone in the university ecosystem.

Easy Schedule Creation

Create and modify timetables with our intuitive drag-and-drop interface. Add courses, assign rooms, and set time slots in minutes.

Conflict Detection

Automatically detect scheduling conflicts for faculty, rooms, and students before finalizing the timetable.

Mobile Access

Access your timetable on any device. Our responsive design works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Real-time Notifications

Get instant notifications about schedule changes, room changes, or cancellations directly to your device.

Analytics & Reports

Generate reports on room utilization, faculty workload, and student attendance patterns to optimize resources.

Integration Ready

Seamlessly integrate with existing university systems like student information systems and learning management platforms.

Conflict Detection

Automatically identify and resolve scheduling conflicts

Our advanced algorithm scans timetables for potential conflicts before they become problems. The system checks for faculty double-bookings, room availability issues, and student schedule overlaps.

Example Conflict Detected:

Professor Johnson is scheduled to teach CS101 in Room A101 and MATH201 in Room B205 at the same time (Monday 9:00-10:30).

How It Works

Real-time Detection

Conflicts are identified as you build the timetable, not after it's completed

Smart Suggestions

Receive intelligent recommendations for resolving detected conflicts

Multiple Conflict Types

Detects faculty, room, and student conflicts with detailed explanations

Conflict History

Track and review previously resolved conflicts for future planning
